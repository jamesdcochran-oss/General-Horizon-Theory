What’s the Horizon Principle?

When flow (energy, matter, or information) pushes past capacity, systems form horizons—boundaries that protect causal order.

Coffee cup brim = a simple horizon. Overflow marks the limit.

Where does it show up?

  • Black holes → event horizon
  • Fog & optics → smoky light wall
  • Plasma → sheaths/shocks
  • Networks & traffic → congestion
Jam front = movement horizon.

Prediction: the practical superpower

If you can estimate Flow and Capacity, you can forecast horizon formation.

Heuristic:  if  Flow / Capacity  ≳ 1  →  Horizon will form
Mass packed tight → event horizon.
Buffer full → information horizon.
Variables: Flow = per-time push; Capacity = safe carry; Horizon = boundary when Flow ≳ Capacity.
